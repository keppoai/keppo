## Best fit

Use LinkedIn when the workflow depends on an approved LinkedIn API surface but you still want Keppo approvals, audit trails, and workspace-scoped credentials:

- community management and outbound posting
- recruiting or talent-system follow-up
- sales or lead-processing workflows
- ads and campaign operations

## What to expect

- LinkedIn in Keppo is a low-level request/response provider, not a hand-modeled tool catalog for every endpoint family.
- Your LinkedIn app may not be approved for every product family. Connect success only proves the app can complete the OAuth flow and use the scopes it was actually granted.
- Write requests should stay approval-gated until the team is comfortable with the exact payloads being sent.

## Common first flow

1. Connect LinkedIn in the workspace.
2. Verify the app has approval for the specific LinkedIn product family you need.
3. Start with `linkedin.getProfile` or a narrow `linkedin.readApi` request.
4. Add `linkedin.writeApi` only after you have reviewed the target payload shape and approval copy.
