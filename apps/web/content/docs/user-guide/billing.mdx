## Billing in practice

Billing matters in two ways:

- your Keppo plan controls the product surface available to the workspace
- Stripe-backed flows can appear inside automations or support workflows

## What operators should watch

- Which team owns the subscription and usage budget
- Whether billing automations still require approval
- Whether the workspace’s automation volume matches the chosen plan

## Best practice

Keep financial writes obvious and reviewable. Approval automation can come later, but the initial rollout should preserve a clear human checkpoint for refunds, subscription changes, or usage-sensitive actions.
